About Us

Pushpak DyeChem, founded by Mr. Nanvit Loya in 1997, is now a nation wide manufacturer and supplier of Sulfur dyes(Navsulf dyes). The headquarters of the company are situated in Wadghadi, Masjid Bundar,Mumbai while the chief manufacturing factory is in Bhiwandi, Mumbai.
