NavSulf Dyes(Sulfur Dyes - Powder & Liquid)
Our high-quality sulfur dyes are ideal for deep, rich shades on cellulose fabrics like cotton and viscose. Best suited for denim applications, these dyes are known for their excellent wash and light fastness, offering cost-effective performance for bulk dyeing. Available in both powder and liquid forms, they ensure easy handling and consistent results across applications—from yarn dyeing to fabric finishing.


Pigment emulsions:
We offer premium-grade pigment emulsions ideal for textile printing applications. These water-based dispersions provide vibrant, long-lasting color on a variety of fabrics with excellent fastness properties. Designed for ease of use in both screen and rotary printing, our pigment emulsions ensure sharp prints, smooth flow, and consistent performance across batches—making them a reliable choice for printers and processors alike.

VAT dyes:
Known as the gold standard for fastness, our VAT dyes deliver exceptional durability on cellulose fabrics like cotton and viscose. Offering brilliant, long-lasting shades, they stand up to repeated washing, sunlight, and even bleaching processes. Ideal for high-end applications where performance is non-negotiable, VAT dyes are the go-to choice when both brightness and staying power matter.

Reactive dyes:
Our reactive dyes are known for their vibrant shades, strong fiber bonding, and excellent colorfastness on natural fibers like cotton and rayon. They are widely favored for their versatility and ease of use, requiring relatively simple dyeing procedures compared to other classes of dyes. Ideal for both batch and continuous dyeing processes, reactive dyes deliver consistent results across a range of textile applications.
